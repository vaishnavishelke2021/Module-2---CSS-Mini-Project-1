Reseach Center

Reseach Center

Research is the fountain of knowledge and an important source of providing guidelines for solving different business, governmental and social problems. To create research awareness amongst our faculty members and students department of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering has offered Ph.D. Course from 2012 under Faculty of Engineering of Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune. The objective of Research Center is give an overview of the recent trends and research development in various fields of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering such as Communication, Signal Processing, VLSI & Embedded systems etc. The department has well equipped laboratories with advanced instruments and latest computing tools to fulfil the need of researchers. Different researchers from Savitribai Phule Pune University are affiliated to the research center to guide the Ph.D students.
