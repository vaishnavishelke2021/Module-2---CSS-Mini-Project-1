Tourist Places

Navshya Ganpati Temple

Category : Religious

The temple is famous for being very
‘Jagrut Devasthan’ and the devotee’s wishes
are fulfilled and hence got the name as ‘Navashya Ganapati’.

Trimbakeshwar Temple

Category : Religious

Tryambakeshwar Shiva Temple is an ancient Hindu temple in the town of Trimbak, in the Trimbakeshwar tehsil in the Nashik District of Maharashtra, India,

Kapaleshwar Temple

Category : Religious

Shree Kapaleshwar Mahadev Mandir, located near Ramkund in Nashik, Maharashtra is one of the prominent Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva in India.

Muktidham Temple

Category : Religious

An abode of most of the gods and goddesses of the Hindu religion, this splendid shrine displays the replica of all the 12 Jyotirlingas of India.

Panchavati

Category : Religious

Holy land for the believers of Ramayana, Panchvati attracts a lot of pilgrims. A serene town located near Nasik, the site offers small sightings having a lot of significance in the epic of Ramayana.

Pandav Leni Caves

Category : Religious

These caves are located on hill at the outskirts of Nashik city on Nashik Mumbai road (NH3) Dadasaheb Phalke smarak is erected at the foots of this hill.

Mangi Tungi Temple

Category : Religious

Mangi-Tungi is a prominent twin-pinnacled peak with plateau in between, located near Tahrabad about 125 km from Nashik, Maharashtra, India.

Someshwar

Category : Religious

Shree Someshwar Mandir is one among the famous temples dedicated to Lord Shiva in the city of Nasik.
The temple lies peacefully on the bank of Indian
sacred River Godavari.

Anjaneri Hills

Category : Religious

Anjaneri is in the mountain range near Nasik about 20 km from the city. It is considered the birthplace of Lord Hanuman and is named after his mother Anjani.

Sundarnarayan Temple

Category : Religious

Sundar Narayan temple is very beautiful and situated near holkar bridge. The temple is dedicated to Lord Vishnu and famous for its intricate carvings.

Chambhar Leni

Category : Religious

Chamber Leni Caves are around 7 KM away from Ramshej Fort. The caves are located off the Nasik Gujarat highway. One can locate the Caves from a distance.

Artillery Museum

Category : Religious

Asia's biggest artillery museum, the Artillary Museum at Deolali Gaon, Nashik, houses a number of vintage and modern weapons including army tanks
